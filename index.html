ColorDNA Privacy Policy Last Updated: June 30, 2026 Privacy Policy Thank you for using ColorDNA. ColorDNA is designed with privacy in mind. The extension performs its functionality locally within your browser and does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal information with third parties. Information Stored Color Library The extension stores: • Colors you manually pick • Website palette colors you choose to save • Favorite colors • Recent color history This information is stored locally on your device using Chrome Storage (chrome.storage.local). Preferences The extension stores your preferences, including: • Theme (Light, Dark, or System) • Auto-copy setting • Auto-save setting • Palette analysis preferences • Library preferences These settings remain on your device and are never transmitted to external servers. Permissions Used Storage Used to save: • Favorite colors • Recent colors • User preferences • Extension settings All information is stored locally in your browser. Active Tab Used only when you explicitly interact with ColorDNA. This permission allows the extension to access the currently active webpage for: • Color picking • Website palette analysis • Typography inspection • Component inspection The extension does not continuously monitor your browsing activity. Scripting Used to temporarily inject the extension's scripts into the active webpage after you request an action. These scripts are required to: • Analyze website colors • Extract color palettes • Inspect typography • Inspect website components Clipboard Write Used only when you choose to copy a color or enable automatic copy after picking a color. Supported formats include: • HEX • RGB • HSL • OKLCH Data Processing All processing is performed locally within your browser. ColorDNA does not: • Collect personal information • Upload your browsing activity • Upload website content • Use analytics services • Track users • Sell user information • Share user information with third parties Third-Party Services ColorDNA does not use: • Analytics platforms • Advertising networks • Remote servers • User tracking technologies Data Retention Your saved colors and preferences remain stored until you choose to remove them. You can delete your data at any time by: • Removing individual colors • Clearing Recent Colors • Clearing Favorites • Resetting the extension • Uninstalling ColorDNA Children's Privacy ColorDNA is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Changes to This Privacy Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated as the extension evolves. Any updates will be reflected on this page. Contact If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or ColorDNA, you can contact the developer.
