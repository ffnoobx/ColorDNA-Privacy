Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 30, 2026

Overview

Thank you for using ColorDNA. ColorDNA is designed with privacy in mind. The extension performs all of its functionality locally inside your browser and does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal information.

Information Stored

Color Library

  • Picked colors
  • Saved website palette colors
  • Favorite colors
  • Recent color history

This information is stored locally using Chrome Storage (chrome.storage.local).


Preferences

  • Theme
  • Auto Copy
  • Auto Save
  • Palette settings
  • Library settings

Permissions Used

Storage

Stores colors, settings and preferences locally.


Active Tab

Used only when you explicitly use ColorDNA to inspect or analyze the current webpage.


Scripting

Temporarily injects ColorDNA's analysis scripts into the active webpage after a user action.


Clipboard Write

Copies color values to your clipboard only when requested.

Data Processing

ColorDNA performs all processing locally.

  • No personal information is collected.
  • No browsing history is uploaded.
  • No website content is stored remotely.
  • No analytics are used.
  • No tracking is performed.
  • No user data is sold.
  • No user data is shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

ColorDNA does not use analytics services, advertising networks, tracking technologies or remote servers.

Data Retention

Your saved colors and settings remain on your device until you choose to remove them.

  • Delete individual colors
  • Clear Recent Colors
  • Clear Favorites
  • Reset the extension
  • Uninstall ColorDNA

Children's Privacy

ColorDNA is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
